Peer.do

a laboratory for co-funded action

We’re evolving simple, accessible tools for crowdfunding that take advantage of the de-central, anonymous, frictionless and low-overhead nature of cryptos.

Peer currencies — starting but not ending with Bitcoin — point the way to new directions in funding, together, the things we want make happen.

We call it co-funding. Peer.do is a laboratory for co-funded action.
